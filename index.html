About

Paul Babba started his pizza store 12yrs ago in his little town of basil, Italy. There he perfected his well known smoked pizza sauce, which only he and a select few family members know the recipe

Babba owes the success of his franchise to his devoted customers. He tries to thank them by creating new sauces and dishes monthly.
